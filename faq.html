Our Services

What types of aquatic services do you provide?

We specialize in three core areas of luxury aquatic services:

  • Private Estate Lifeguarding: Professional safety oversight for family gatherings, events, and ongoing residential protection
  • Bespoke Swimming Instruction: Personalized lessons for all ages and abilities, including special needs and Olympic-level technique training
  • Luxury Pool & Event Management: Comprehensive aquatic safety and service management for corporate events, weddings, and private celebrations

Each service is tailored to meet the specific needs and privacy requirements of distinguished estates throughout Southern California.

Do you provide services for special needs swimmers?

Absolutely. Jasper has specialized training and extensive experience working with special needs swimmers of all ages. Our adaptive swimming programs are designed to:

  • Build water confidence in a safe, supportive environment
  • Develop swimming skills adapted to individual abilities
  • Provide patient, encouraging instruction tailored to each swimmer's unique needs
  • Create positive water experiences that build independence and joy

We work closely with families to understand specific requirements and create customized programs that celebrate each swimmer's progress.

What age ranges do you work with for swimming instruction?

We provide swimming instruction for all ages, from toddlers to adults (ages 2-99). Our programs are specifically tailored for:

  • Toddlers (2-4 years): Water confidence and basic safety skills
  • Children (5-12 years): Fundamental stroke development and swimming proficiency
  • Teens (13-17 years): Advanced technique, competitive training, and performance optimization
  • Adults (18+ years): Beginner confidence building, stroke refinement, and fitness swimming

Multi-generational family programs are also available, allowing families to learn and progress together.

Can you provide lifeguarding services for large events?

Yes, we specialize in aquatic safety management for events of all sizes, from intimate family gatherings to large corporate retreats with 200+ guests. Our event services include:

  • Pre-event safety assessment and risk mitigation planning
  • Professional lifeguard coverage during your event
  • Coordination with catering, event staff, and security teams
  • Emergency response protocols tailored to your venue
  • Discrete, professional presentation that enhances your event

We've successfully managed aquatic safety for Fortune 500 corporate retreats, luxury weddings, charity galas, and high-profile private celebrations.

Do you offer ongoing residential lifeguarding services?

Yes, we provide ongoing residential lifeguarding services for families who want consistent, professional aquatic safety at their private estates. This service is ideal for:

  • Families with young children who use their pool regularly
  • Multi-generational estates with varying swimming abilities
  • Properties that frequently host guests and events
  • Families seeking peace of mind and professional oversight

We can arrange flexible scheduling including daily, weekly, or seasonal coverage based on your family's lifestyle and needs.

Booking & Scheduling

How far in advance should I book your services?

Booking timeline recommendations vary by service and season:

  • Swimming Instruction: 1-2 weeks for regular lessons, 3-4 weeks for intensive programs
  • Event Lifeguarding: 2-4 weeks for optimal scheduling, earlier for peak season events
  • Emergency/Last-Minute: We accommodate urgent requests when possible (48-hour notice preferred)
  • Seasonal Services: 4-6 weeks advance booking recommended for summer season

Peak times (summer months, holidays) book earliest, so early planning ensures your preferred dates and times.

What information do you need to provide a quote?

To provide an accurate, customized quote, we need:

  • Service Type: Lifeguarding, instruction, or event management
  • Location: Estate address and pool/venue details
  • Dates & Times: Preferred schedule and duration
  • Participants: Number of people, ages, and skill levels
  • Special Requirements: Accessibility needs, special circumstances
  • Event Details: Type of gathering, guest count, other vendors

The more details you provide, the more accurate and comprehensive our proposal will be.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair while protecting our ability to serve all clients:

  • Weather Cancellations: No charge for cancellations due to unsafe weather conditions
  • Emergency Situations: Handled case-by-case with maximum flexibility

We understand that plans change and work with clients to find mutually acceptable solutions whenever possible.
