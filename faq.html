Services & Offerings

What types of aquatic services do you provide?

We offer comprehensive luxury aquatic services including:

  • Private Estate Lifeguarding: Certified safety professionals for your family's peace of mind
  • Bespoke Swimming Instruction: Personalized lessons for all ages and skill levels
  • Luxury Event Management: Discrete aquatic safety for private events and gatherings
  • Water Safety Consultation: Professional assessment and protocol development
  • Special Needs Programs: Adaptive swimming and therapeutic aquatic services

Each service is customized to meet the unique requirements of your estate and family.

Do you provide services for special needs individuals?

Yes, we specialize in adaptive swimming and therapeutic aquatic programs. Our founder has extensive training in special needs instruction and has successfully worked with individuals with various challenges including:

  • Autism spectrum disorders
  • Physical disabilities
  • Developmental delays
  • Water anxiety and phobias
  • Sensory processing differences

We create individualized programs that focus on comfort, safety, and gradual skill building in a supportive environment.

Can you provide services for large events?

Absolutely. We specialize in luxury event management with discrete aquatic safety oversight. Our event services include:

  • Corporate retreats and executive gatherings
  • Private parties and celebrations
  • Wedding receptions and milestone events
  • Charity galas and fundraising events
  • Media productions and photo shoots

We coordinate with your event planning team to ensure seamless integration of safety protocols without disrupting the event's atmosphere.

What areas do you service?

Our primary service area includes the greater Conejo Valley and surrounding luxury communities:

Primary Areas: Thousand Oaks, Westlake Village, Agoura Hills, Newbury Park, Oak Park

Extended Coverage: Malibu, Calabasas, Hidden Hills, Camarillo, Ventura

Premium Locations: Beverly Hills, Manhattan Beach, Newport Beach, Palos Verdes, Laguna Beach

We also provide services to other distinguished locations throughout Southern California. Contact us to discuss your specific location.

Do you offer swimming lessons for adults?

Yes, we provide comprehensive adult swimming programs tailored to various goals:

  • Executive Fitness: Swimming for health and fitness goals
  • Beginner Programs: Learn to swim in a private, comfortable environment
  • Technique Refinement: Improve stroke efficiency and speed
  • Competition Preparation: Training for triathlons or swimming competitions
  • Anxiety Management: Overcome water fears in a supportive setting

All adult programs are conducted with complete discretion and flexible scheduling to accommodate busy executive lifestyles.

Safety & Certification

What certifications do your lifeguards hold?

Our team maintains the highest professional certifications:

  • American Red Cross Lifeguarding Certification
  • CPR/AED Certification
  • First Aid Certification
  • Water Safety Instructor (WSI) Certification
  • EMT Certification (select personnel)

All certifications are current and we participate in ongoing training to maintain the highest standards of aquatic safety.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, 805 LifeGuard is fully licensed, bonded, and insured with comprehensive coverage including:

  • General liability insurance
  • Professional liability coverage
  • Bonding for high-value estate services
  • Workers' compensation insurance

We can provide certificates of insurance and bonding documentation upon request for your estate management team.

What background checks do you perform?

We understand the importance of security for our high-profile clientele. Our background verification includes:

  • Comprehensive criminal background checks
  • Reference verification from previous employers
  • Identity verification and documentation review
  • Driving record checks
  • Social media and online presence screening

Additional security clearance can be arranged for celebrity and executive protection level requirements.

Pricing & Packages

How is pricing determined for your services?

Our pricing is customized based on several factors:

  • Service Type: Lifeguarding, instruction, event management, or consultation
  • Duration: Session length, seasonal contracts, or ongoing relationships
  • Estate Size & Complexity: Pool facilities, property size, and accessibility
  • Guest Count: Number of participants or event attendees
  • Scheduling: Peak times, holidays, and urgency requirements
  • Special Requirements: Security clearance, specialized equipment, or unique protocols

We provide detailed, transparent proposals during our consultation process with no hidden fees.

Do you offer package deals or retainer agreements?

Yes, we offer several advantageous arrangements for ongoing clients:

  • Seasonal Packages: Comprehensive coverage for pool season with preferred rates
  • Monthly Retainers: Priority scheduling and dedicated service availability
  • Annual Contracts: Year-round aquatic management with significant savings
  • Multi-Service Bundles: Combined lifeguarding, instruction, and event services
  • Family Programs: Multi-generational instruction packages

Retainer clients receive priority scheduling, emergency response, and preferred pricing on additional services.

Booking & Scheduling

How far in advance should I book services?

Booking recommendations vary by service type and season:

  • Regular Swimming Lessons: 1-2 weeks for optimal scheduling
  • Private Lifeguarding: 2-4 weeks for peak season, 1 week off-season
  • Event Services: 4-8 weeks for large events, 2 weeks for intimate gatherings
  • Emergency/Last-Minute: 24-48 hours when availability permits
  • Holiday Weekends: 6-8 weeks advance booking recommended

Retainer clients receive priority access and can often secure services with shorter notice.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair while protecting our scheduling integrity:

  • 48+ Hours Notice: Full refund or rescheduling
  • 24-48 Hours Notice: 50% fee applies
  • Less than 24 Hours: Full session fee due
  • Weather Cancellations: Full rescheduling at no charge
  • Emergency Situations: Considered case-by-case with discretion

Events and multi-day services have specific cancellation terms outlined in the service agreement.
