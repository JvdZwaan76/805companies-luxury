Exclusive Pool Maintenance

Who can access your pool cleaning services?

Our pool cleaning services are exclusively available to current 805 LifeGuard clients who utilize our lifeguarding or swimming instruction services. This ensures consistent quality standards and integrated service coordination.

Our comprehensive service includes:

  • •Equipment Maintenance: Filter cleaning, pump inspection
  • •Detailed Reporting: Comprehensive service reports
