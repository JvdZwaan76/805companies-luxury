Frequently Asked Questions

The questions our clients — and their estate managers, HOA boards, and legal counsel — most commonly ask before service commencement.

What insurance coverage does 805 LifeGuard carry?

805 LifeGuard carries $1,000,000 per occurrence general liability insurance plus $5,000,000 excess liability coverage. All policies cover private residences, HOA community pools, corporate venues, and luxury events throughout Southern California. We are fully licensed and bonded for private estate services in California.

Can my HOA or property management company be named as additional insured?

Yes. HOA boards, property management companies, and homeowners can all be named as additional insured on our $1,000,000 general liability policy at no additional cost. The $5,000,000 excess liability is also available for communities requiring enhanced protection. Insurance certificates are issued and provided before service commencement.

Does 805 LifeGuard insurance cover private pool parties at my home?

Yes. Our insurance covers all services at private residences — pool parties, family events, daily lifeguard coverage, swimming instruction, and corporate entertaining. Property damage and personal injury are both included under our general liability policy. Your homeowner's insurance does not need to be your first line of defence when a professional aquatic service is on your property. Ours is.

What certifications do 805 LifeGuard professionals hold?

All professionals hold current American Red Cross Lifeguard Certification, CPR/AED for Professional Rescuers, and Water Safety Instructor (WSI) certification. Additional credentials include Special Needs Aquatic Instruction certification, California business licensing, professional bonding, and comprehensive criminal background check verification. All certifications are renewed on schedule without exception.

How quickly can 805 LifeGuard provide insurance documentation?

All insurance documentation — certificates of insurance, additional insured endorsements, bonding certificates, and certification copies — is provided before service commencement. For HOA board presentations or legal counsel review, contact us at (805) 367-6432 and we will prepare a complete credentials package tailored to your requirements.

What areas does 805 LifeGuard serve?

805 LifeGuard provides fully insured aquatic services throughout Southern California, including Thousand Oaks, Westlake Village, Agoura Hills, Newbury Park, Calabasas, Hidden Hills, Malibu, Beverly Hills, Manhattan Beach, Newport Beach, Palos Verdes, and Laguna Beach. Contact us to discuss service availability for your location.
