Privacy Commitment

At 805 LifeGuard, we understand that privacy and discretion are paramount for our distinguished clientele. This Privacy Policy outlines our unwavering commitment to protecting your personal information and maintaining the highest standards of confidentiality in all our interactions.

As providers of luxury aquatic services to high-profile families and estates, we recognize that trust is the foundation of our relationship with you. This policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your personal information in accordance with applicable privacy laws and our own rigorous standards.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our premium aquatic services and ensure the safety and satisfaction of your family. This information is collected through direct interactions, service delivery, and our website.

Personal Information

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Emergency contact details
  • Family member information relevant to service delivery
  • Payment and billing information
  • Service preferences and special requirements
  • Health and safety considerations (when relevant to services)

Property Information

  • Property security and access protocols

Technical Information

  • Website usage data and analytics
  • IP address and browser information
  • Communication preferences
  • Service history and appointment records
  • Feedback and service evaluation data

Your information is used exclusively to deliver exceptional aquatic services and maintain the high standards of safety and professionalism our clients expect. We never use your information for purposes unrelated to our service relationship.

Billing & Payment

Information Sharing & Disclosure

Confidentiality Guarantee

We Do Not Sell, Rent, or Trade Your Personal Information

Your privacy is absolutely protected. We never sell, rent, lease, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes or any other commercial use.

Information sharing is limited to specific circumstances necessary for service delivery and legal compliance:

Service Providers

Trusted partners who assist in service delivery (payment processors, scheduling systems) under strict confidentiality agreements

Emergency Situations

Medical or safety emergencies where disclosure is necessary to protect health and safety

Legal Requirements

When required by law, court order, or regulatory authority (with notice when legally permitted)

Business Transfers

In the unlikely event of business sale or merger, with continued privacy protection guarantees

Data Security Measures

Protecting your information is our top priority. We employ comprehensive security measures that meet or exceed industry standards, including those used for high-security and celebrity protection services.

Encryption & Access Control

  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest and in transit
  • Multi-factor authentication for all system access
  • Role-based access control with minimum necessary permissions
  • Regular access reviews and permission audits

Infrastructure Security

  • Secure cloud hosting with redundant backups
  • Network monitoring and intrusion detection
  • Regular security assessments and penetration testing
  • Secure communication channels for all data transmission

Staff Training & Protocols

  • Comprehensive privacy and security training for all personnel
  • Non-disclosure agreements for all staff and contractors
  • Background checks for personnel with data access
  • Regular privacy and security awareness updates

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Retention periods vary based on the type of information and purpose for which it was collected.

Financial Information
