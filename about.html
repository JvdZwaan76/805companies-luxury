Our Founder

Jasper van der Zwaan

Founder & Lead Instructor

From competitive swimming in his youth to becoming Southern California's most trusted private aquatic safety professional, Jasper's journey has been defined by an unwavering commitment to excellence and a passion for water safety education.

Founded in 2016, 805 LifeGuard began with a simple yet ambitious mission: to provide the same level of professional aquatic safety services that Jasper had experienced during his training with elite swimming programs, but tailored specifically for private clientele and exclusive events.

Professional Achievements & Certifications

  • American Red Cross Lifeguard Certification (Current)
  • CPR/AED for Professional Rescuers
  • Water Safety Instructor (WSI) Certification
  • Licensed & Bonded for Private Property Services
  • Special Needs Aquatic Instruction Specialist
  • Background Checked & Security Cleared
  • 7+ Years Serving Premium Clientele
  • 100+ Successful Private Events
"Every family deserves to enjoy their pool with complete peace of mind. My role isn't just about safety—it's about creating an environment where memories are made, confidence is built, and the joy of swimming can be experienced without worry." — Jasper van der Zwaan, Founder

What sets Jasper apart is his unique combination of technical expertise and interpersonal skills. His experience working with children of all ages and abilities, including those with special needs, has earned him the trust of some of the most discerning families in Southern California.

When not providing aquatic services, Jasper continues his education in water safety techniques, participates in Red Cross instructor training programs, and mentors new lifeguards entering the profession.
