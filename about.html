Meet Our Founder

Jasper van der Zwaan

Founder & Lead Aquatic Safety Specialist

With over seven years of dedicated experience in aquatic safety and instruction, Jasper founded 805 LifeGuard with a simple yet profound mission: to provide unparalleled water safety services with the discretion and professionalism that discerning families deserve.

Born from a passion for water safety and a deep understanding of the unique needs of high-profile families, Jasper has developed a reputation for excellence that extends far beyond traditional lifeguarding services. His expertise encompasses not only emergency response but also personalized swimming instruction, special needs aquatic therapy, and luxury event management.

Professional Achievements

  • American Red Cross Certified Lifeguard & Swimming Instructor
  • Specialized training in special needs aquatic therapy
  • Licensed, insured, and bonded professional
  • 7+ years of experience with high-profile families
  • Expert in emergency response and water safety protocols
  • Multilingual capabilities for international clients

What sets Jasper apart is not just his technical expertise, but his genuine care for each family he serves. He understands that every client has unique needs, whether it's helping a child overcome water anxiety, providing Olympic-level stroke refinement, or ensuring the safety of guests at an exclusive estate event.
