Meet Our Founder

Jasper van der Zwaan

Founder & Lead Aquatic Safety Specialist

Jasper's journey into luxury aquatic services began with a simple belief: every family deserves to feel completely safe and confident around water, without compromising their privacy or lifestyle.

With over 8 years of experience in aquatic safety and instruction, Jasper has built 805 LifeGuard into Southern California's most trusted name in premium pool services. His approach combines world-class safety protocols with the discretion and white-glove service expected by discerning families.

What sets Jasper apart is his genuine passion for helping people of all ages and abilities develop confidence in the water. He specializes in working with special needs swimmers and has helped hundreds of families create lasting memories around their pools—safely and joyfully.
